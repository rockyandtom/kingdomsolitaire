How to Play

Kingdom Solitaire combines classic solitaire mechanics with kingdom-building strategy. Your goal is to clear the board while collecting resources and building your medieval kingdom.

Here's how to get started:

  • Match cards that are one value higher or lower than your currently selected card
  • Collect special cards to gain resources for your kingdom
  • Use strategic planning to clear difficult board layouts
  • Progress through increasingly challenging levels

Game Controls

  • Mouse/Touch: Click or tap on cards to select and match them
  • Undo: Use the undo button to reverse your last move
  • Hints: Click the hint button if you're stuck
  • Menu: Access settings and game options

About Kingdom Solitaire

Kingdom Solitaire is a unique card game that combines the familiar mechanics of solitaire with strategic kingdom-building elements. Set in a medieval fantasy world, players must match cards strategically while collecting resources to construct and expand their kingdom.

Unlike traditional solitaire games, Kingdom Solitaire introduces special cards, power-ups, and obstacles that create a deeper and more engaging gameplay experience. Each level presents new challenges and opportunities to advance your kingdom.

Whether you're a solitaire enthusiast or new to card games, Kingdom Solitaire offers accessible gameplay with increasing depth and strategy as you progress. The game features beautiful artwork, satisfying sound effects, and smooth animations that make every card match rewarding.
